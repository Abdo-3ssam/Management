The **Management Dashboard** is a comprehensive **Power BI solution** designed for businesses to track, analyze, and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) in real-time. The dashboard provides managers, analysts, and stakeholders with a clear understanding of **financial performance, property sales, client engagement**, and other critical business metrics.  

With this dashboard, users can make informed decisions, identify trends, and monitor business health efficiently.

---

## ğŸ¯ Key Features

- **Revenue Insights**: Analyze revenue by **month**, **type**, **country**, and **sales channels**, enabling a deep understanding of income sources.  
- **Expense & Income Tracking**: Compare **expenses vs. income** visually to identify cost-saving opportunities and profitability trends.  
- **Property Sales Overview**: Monitor properties sold with detailed statuses such as **Paid, Pending, or Overdue**, including sale date and amount.  
- **Client Analytics**: Identify top clients, their contributions, and trends in client engagement for better relationship management.  
- **Interactive Visualizations**: Rich charts, tables, and geographic maps allow users to **interact with data dynamically** for intuitive exploration.  
- **Dynamic Filtering**: Use slicers to filter by **year**, **location**, **property type**, or **sales channel** for detailed insights.  
- **Trend Analysis**: Track **monthly revenue trends**, identify high-performing sales channels, and forecast business growth.  
- **Custom KPI Metrics**: Highlight key performance metrics such as **top clients, highest revenue regions**, and **most profitable property types**.

## ğŸ—‚ Dashboard Sections

1. **Revenue & Expenses Summary**: Provides a **quick snapshot** of total revenue, expenses, and net income to assess financial health at a glance.
